# Product Management and Design Skills

## Overview
Skills for product discovery, strategy, design thinking, and customer research.

## Discovery and Research

### discovery-interview-prep
**Purpose**: Plan customer discovery interviews with the right goal, segment, constraints
**When to use**: Preparing for customer interviews
**Dependencies**: None
**Related**: discovery-process, jobs-to-be-done
**Domain**: Customer research

### discovery-process
**Purpose**: Run a full discovery cycle from problem hypothesis to validated solution
**When to use**: When you need to validate a problem or solution
**Dependencies**: None
**Related**: discovery-interview-prep, pol-probe
**Domain**: Product discovery

### jobs-to-be-done
**Purpose**: Uncover customer jobs, pains, and gains in a structured JTBD format
**When to use**: When clarifying customer needs
**Dependencies**: None
**Related**: discovery-process, user-story
**Domain**: JTBD framework

### customer-journey-map
**Purpose**: Create a customer journey map across stages, touchpoints, actions, emotions
**When to use**: Mapping customer experience
**Dependencies**: None
**Related**: customer-journey-mapping-workshop
**Domain**: Journey mapping

### customer-journey-mapping-workshop
**Purpose**: Run a customer journey mapping workshop with adaptive questions
**When to use**: Facilitating journey mapping sessions
**Dependencies**: None
**Related**: customer-journey-map, workshop-facilitation
**Domain**: Workshop facilitation
